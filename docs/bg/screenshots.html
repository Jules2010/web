    Screenshots


    Calculate Balance
    Showing a summary of your financial position.
    Correct Balance
    Allows you to set the balance on a specific day from your statement.
    Bills and Deposits
    All your recurring and one off transactions.
    Transactions
    Here you can add or edit transactions, the screen will be labelled Add or Edit.
    Category
    The screen where you can add one of multiple categories at once, edit existing items or select an item.
    Graph Choice
    A selection of graphs and pie charts.
    Balance Forecast chart
    One of the most useful parts of the app, allowing you to instantly see when and where you’ll be on a certain date.
    Spending by Category Pie Chart
    A useful pie chart showing the spending pattern by category.
    Forecast
    This is possibly the most useful screen, it will take your recurring transactions, your one off transaction and produce a forecast, grouping items by days. The filter also allows you to select a date range in the past.
    Forecast Calendar
    This screen shows a forecast by days of the calendar.
